Project Insights and Visualizations.¶

Overview¶

This document is an insights and visualizations report for the Udacity data wrangling project, which involved interacting with tweets data for the WeRateDogs account that rates people's dogs with a humorous comment about the dog. The process of interacting with the tweets data involved gathering the data, that is gathering the data from various sources, assessing the data to view facts about the data, while gathering insights about the data, cleaning the data, and executing a simplistic exploratory data analysis to vizualise some interesting patterns in the cleaned data.

After the wrangling process, I had a couple of wuestions that had to be answered.

  1. What is WeRateDog's monthly trend of tweet posts?
  2. What is the engagement rate with the tweets basing on the favourites and retweets per tweet over the given months?
  3. Which dog stage is retweeted, and favourited the most?

To answer these questions, I came up with the following graphic representations to answer the questions that I had.

1. What is WeRateDog's monthly trend of tweet posts?¶

My investigation of the data after the data wranggling process showed that the number of tweets reduced. There were highs of >300 tweets sometime around mid 2015, and lows <20 tweets in the month of August, 2017. 2. What is the engagement rate with the tweets basing on the favourites and retweets per tweet over the given months?¶

People like dogs, because they are naturally beautiful and caring animals. I asked myself the question of how engaeging these tweets had become over the period of time, and what impact they created socially in terms of rating the dogs, and looking into the enagement rate in terms of favouriting and retweeting the tweets was a place to look at. The investigation rather showed that as time went on, from the period between 2015 and 2017, there was a gradual increase on the engagament/ reaction to the tweets by "tweeps". Initially, there were approximately 2000 reactions mid 2015, however this number gradually increased up to 40000 reactions by August, 2017. Which is an interesting rise in interaction/reaction. 3. Which dog stage is retweeted, and favourited the most?¶

I had to look into the fact that different dog stages drive the attention of the multitude. A quick insight into this question was meant to answer the question of what dog stage would be ideal or liked?. The answer was in the analysis, in which I grouped the dog stage and looked at the mean number of favourites and retweets for each dog stage. The investigation of the mean number of favourites for the dog stage showed that the Puppo, had the highest mean number of favourites, and the pupper had the least mean number of favourites. This can be seen in the graph below.

As for the investigation of the mean number of retweets for the different dog stages, the Doggo on average had the highest number of retweets, and the Pupper had the lowest number of retweets. This is shown below.

Based on the investigative anaylsis, a conclusion can be drawn that a dog within the Pupper stage is not really liked as much.
